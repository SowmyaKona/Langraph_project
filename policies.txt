Notice Period:
Employees must serve a 60-day notice period.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves every year.

Work From Home:
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week.

Laptop Policy:
Every employee is provided with a company laptop.

Attendance Policy:
Employees must maintain at least 90% attendance.

Travel Policy:
Business travel expenses are reimbursed after approval.

Medical Leave:
Employees are eligible for 10 days of medical leave annually.
